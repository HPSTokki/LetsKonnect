SANTA LUCIA SK COUNCIL

Brgy. Sta. Lucia SK Council: Your Youth, Your Voice, Your Future

The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) Council of Barangay Sta. Lucia is committed to empowering the youth and fostering community growth. With a focus on inclusivity, innovation, and leadership, we strive to address the needs and aspirations of our young constituents. Our aim is to build a brighter future where the youth play a pivotal role in shaping our barangay’s progress and success.

Our Mission:
To uplift the lives of the youth in Brgy. Sta. Lucia through meaningful programs, initiatives, and opportunities. By promoting education, sports, arts, and leadership, we aim to create avenues where the potential of every young individual can be realized and celebrated.

Our Vision:
A thriving and empowered youth, united in their dedication to community development and progress. We envision a barangay where young leaders inspire change, advocate for the welfare of others, and contribute to a sustainable and prosperous future for all.

Our Values:
- Integrity: Upholding honesty and transparency in all our initiatives.
- Inclusivity: Embracing diversity and ensuring that every youth has a voice.
- Collaboration: Working hand in hand with the community to achieve shared goals.
- Innovation: Encouraging creativity and forward-thinking to address the challenges of today and tomorrow.

We invite all members of the community to join us in our journey. Together, let’s empower the youth and build a stronger Barangay Sta. Lucia.
